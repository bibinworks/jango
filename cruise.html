House Boats :

KERALA Houseboats or VANCHI VEEDU were traditionally Cargo Boats (KETTUVALLAMS) and acted as the backbone of KUTTANADAN Villages the inland waterways to carry rice and other commodities which made their living colourful, now with industrial effects and developed road the boats where shifted to sheds some submerged into the water. Then in someone’s thoughts came the idea of transforming these Old Kings to something very special and once again to become the back bone of Alleppey. These where transformed into boats with bedrooms, kitchen, living area, bathrooms and other amenities. Today, these allow travellers to enjoy the breath-taking beauty of Alappuzha while cruising along its backwaters.

Shikara boats :

Shikara boats can also described as miniature houseboats. They are traditional motorized country canoe boats with the seating capacity of 4- 25 people. With the bamboo canopy overhead and all four sides open for enthral sightseeing, they are built with elegant Kerala style. Depending upon the capacity the size of the shikara differ now a day, it is an ideal choice to explore the interiors of Alleppey backwaters.

Motor boats :

Motor boats in Alleppey backwaters are primarily used for transportation and also used as an economical alternative to houseboat in Kerala. It is suitable for tourists with both time and budget constrain, but still want to enjoy a cruise along the mesmerizing backwaters of Alleppey.
